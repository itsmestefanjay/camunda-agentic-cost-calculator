About the project

When evaluating whether to automate a process with Camunda's agentic orchestration capabilities, the first questions are always economic: is it worth doing, and which approach fits the budget? These calculators give decision-makers a structured way to answer both quickly.

The goal is a directional answer in minutes — not a replacement for a proper business case, but a concrete starting point that makes the conversation grounded and fast.

The two-step workflow mirrors how good investment decisions are made:

  • Step 1 — build a business case: payback, NPV, go/no-go recommendation
  • Step 2 — find a model that fits your cost envelope and quality bar

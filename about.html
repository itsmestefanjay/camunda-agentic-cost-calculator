Agentic Decision Framework

About the project

When evaluating whether to automate a process with Camunda's agentic orchestration capabilities, the first questions are always economic: is it worth doing, and which approach fits the budget? These tools give decision-makers a structured way to answer both quickly.

The goal is a directional answer in minutes, not a replacement for a proper business case, but a concrete starting point that makes the conversation grounded and fast.

The four-step workflow mirrors how good investment decisions are made:

  • Step 1: Business Case - payback, long-term value, go/no-go recommendation
  • Step 2: Process Prioritization - score and rank candidate processes by value and feasibility
  • Step 3: Model Cost & Fit - compare AI model costs against your volume and quality needs
  • Step 4: Summary - export results as JSON for AI tools, PDF for documents, or share via link
